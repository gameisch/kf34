Frequently Asked Questions
  1. Can an individual register for training without being employed?

    No, an individual must be sent directly from the company with whom they are currently employed.

  2. What kind of equipment do you offer training on?

    Powered Industrial Forklifts: Sit-Down, Stand-Up, Pallet Jacks, Walkie Stackers, Rough Terrain Straight Mast Forklift (Forklift Classification 1 – 7) Aerial Work Platforms: Scissor Lifts (A92.6), Boom Lifts (A92.5), Personnel Lifts (A92.3) Rough Terrain Forklift: Telehandlers (Forklift Classification 7)

  3. Do you offer training for non-English speaking customers?

    Unfortunately, our training program is only offered in English at this time.

  4. How can I register for a class?

    We offer training at our Franklin Park facility as well as on-site training at your facility. Please refer to the calendar on the safety training page of our website to see available classes. Give us a call us at 630-782-1000to confirm availability.

    Online Registering
    Click “Register” located on the Safety Training page of our website Fill out the registration form online with all required contact and payment information Email the completed form to:training@nlt.comOr fax the form to: 630-782-1014 You will receive a confirmation email once the form is submitted and reviewed.

    Phone or Email Inquires
    Give us a call at: 630-782-1000 Email us at:training@nlt.comClick “Schedule Training” located on the safety training page of our website, fill out the form to submit your request and our coordinator will contact you directly to discuss scheduling.

  5. Where is the training location?

    3333 Mt. Prospect Rd. Franklin Park, IL 60131
    Please go to the main entrance, south of the parking lot, and check in with the receptionist on the day of training.

  6. I am unable to attend Thursday classes. Can training be scheduled any day during the week?

    Yes, we will be happy to schedule training any day during the week to accommodate your schedule. We do require at least a week notice to confirm our trainer availability.

  7. How long is the class?

    Depending on the class size, each class will take 4-5 hours to complete.

  8. What is the maximum number of attendees per class?

    There is a 12 person per class maximum for training at National Lift Truck. On-site training class maximums vary by customer requirements.

  9. What do I need to wear or bring to class?

    You will be operating equipment, please use the following as a guideline for safe operation:
    Wear comfortable clothing that is free of anything that may catch on the equipment
    Wear closed-toe shoes
    *Boom lift operation requirement: Please bring a safety harness and tether to class.

  10. How long does it take to receive the certificate packet?

    Please allow 2-4 days after taking the class to receive your certificate packet. If you require documentation immediately, please notify us prior to your scheduled class so we can make the appropriate arrangements.

  11. Will my wallet card be valid at another company?

    No, certificates and wallet cards are non-transferable. Training documents stay with the company who requested your training.

  12. What is the refresher course?

    The refresher course is for experienced operators who are interested in renewing their certification. National Lift Truck requires operators to renew their wallet cards every three years. Supervisors may send their employees in for a refresher course sooner if any of the following occur:

    • Employers observe workers who are operating a lift improperly
    • An accident occurs during equipment use
    • Workplace hazards involving equipment are discovered
    • A different type of lift is being used

    Upon successful completion of both the classroom and hand-on operation of equipment, students will receive a renewed certificate and wallet card.

  13. How long is the “train the trainer” certification good for?

    Trainer status never expires. However, operator status must be renewed once every three years.

  14. I misplaced my Trainer’s Manual. Can I purchase replacement training materials?

    If you successfully completed the “Train the Trainer” program at National Lift Truck and would like to purchase new materials, please give us a call at:630-782-1000
    or E-Mail us at:training@nlt.com
    for more information.
